What Are Synonyms?

A synonym is a word that has the same or nearly the same meaning as another word. Synonyms are used in different contexts and can have slightly different connotations. The word synonymy comes from the Greek meaning "same name."

Why Are Synonyms Useful?

Synonyms can be useful in many ways, such as expanding your verbal and written vocabulary. If your writing is often uninteresting and monotonous, finding synonyms can enchance the quality of your writing. If you often sound repetitive, synonyms can make you sound more intelligent and can help you express yourself better by being more specific which furhter improves your communication skills.
